Gallery

A haunted house gallery is an immersive art space designed to evoke fear and suspense. It combines eerie visuals, dim lighting, unsettling soundscapes, and ghostly elements. Visitors walk through rooms filled with haunting installations, creating a chilling, interactive experience that blurs the line between art and horror.
